# 元语言核心
是不是意思是x "does it mean x"
x是什么意思 "what does x mean"
你能举个例子吗 "can you give an example"
有什么区别 "what's the difference"
我说得对吗 "did I say it right"
我发音对吗 "is my pronunciation correct"
这样说自然吗 "is it natural to say it this way"
“<单词或想法>”用中文怎么说 "how to say <word or idea> in chinese"
“<物体或概念>”的中文是什么 "what is the chinese for <object or concept>"
你能再说一遍吗 "can you say it again"
你能说慢一点吗 "can you speak slower"
你能纠正我吗 "can you correct me"
我哪里说错了 "what did i say wrong"
我该怎么正确地说 "how should i say it correctly"
x还有别的说法吗 "other ways to say x"
x的反义词是什么 "what is the opposite of x"
你能写“<单词>”吗 "can you write <word>"
你能念“<单词>”吗 "can you read <word> aloud"
你能拼“<单词>”吗 "can you spell <word>"
你能翻译“<单词或短语>”吗 "can you translate <word or phrase>"
你明白吗 "do you understand"
我明白 understand
我不明白 "i do not understand"
是 yes
不是 no
也许 maybe
