chinese to english dictionary

about
  • search results are sorted by word frequency and word length
  • for latin character input, searches in pinyin and optionally in translations if the checkbox translations is checked. the partial checkbox has no effect
  • for chinese character input, splits into words and searches for exact matches. if the checkbox partial is checked, does not split into words and searches for partial matches. the checkbox translations has no effect
  • supports regexp syntax except for chinese characters without the checkbox partial checked. for example, ^word for searching at the beginning, word$ for searching at the end, or . for wildcards
  • result word length is limited based on the length of input
  • results can be directly copied and saved as a machine-readable space-separated dsv file that can be opened as a spreadsheet
  • word frequency from ernop/anki-chinese-word-frequency
  • translations from cc-cedict
