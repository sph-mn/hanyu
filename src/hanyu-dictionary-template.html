about
word search
  • includes over 60000 translations, sorted by frequency of use; not intended as a comprehensive dictionary
  • excludes some less common categories, such as regional variants, archaic forms, and specialized vocabulary
  • results are ranked by frequency and word length
  • for latin input, searches match pinyin and optionally translations if the "translations" checkbox is selected; the "split" checkbox is ignored
  • for chinese input, partial matches are returned; with "split" checked, input is segmented and exact matches are searched; "translations" is ignored
  • supports regular expressions, unless "split" is enabled for chinese input; "^word" matches word starts, "word$" matches word ends, "." is a wildcard
  • output may be truncated based on input length
  • results can be copied as space-separated DSV, readable by spreadsheets
  • frequency data is based on SUBTLEX-CH
  • translations are sourced from cc-cedict
  • project homepage
character lookup
  • search by character or pinyin, with numeric tone marks
  • comma-separate multiple queries
  • "containing" and "contained" checkboxes have no effect for latin input
  • graphics from animcjk
general
  • supports only simplified characters
